Find Lift Coefficient of an Arbitrary Airfoil
This program calculates the mean camber line (show in red) from the airfoil draw then uses thin airfoil theory to find the lift coefficient.

Instructions: Draw the airfoil within the box provided, where the dashed line is the cord. If you wish todo another airfoil a page refresh is required. Results obtained are purely for fun.
